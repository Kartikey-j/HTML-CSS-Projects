Paneer tikka

Ingredients :

  • 1/2 cup Yogurt
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp kashmiri red Chilli powder
  • 1/2 tsp Coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp Garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp Kasuri methi
  • 1/2 tsp Chaat masala
  • 1 tsp Ginger Garlic paste
  • 1/4 tsp Carom seeds
  • 2 tsp Gram flour
  • 1 tbsp Lemon juice
  • Salt to taste
  • 3 tsp Oil

Vegetables -

  • 1/2 Onions
  • 1/2 Capscum (red & green)
  • 5 cubes Paneer

Instructions :

  1. Take 1/2 cup Yogurt.
  2. Add all the spices along with Salt.
  3. Mix everything well with Curd.
  4. Now add 1/2 Onion, 1/2 cubed Capsicum and 5 cubes Paneer.
  5. Also add 1 tsp of Oil.
  6. Mix gently till all the vegetables are coated well.
  7. To marinate, cover and refrigerate it for 30 minutes.
  8. After marination, insert the marinated Paneer, Capsicum and Onions into wooden Skewers.
  9. Roast it on a hot Tawa or grill in Oven.
  10. Finally, sprinkle some Chaat masala and serve Paneer tikka immediately.
