Cheese burst Pizza

Ingredients :

For Pizza dough -

  • 1/2 cup Water (warm)
  • 1 tsp Sugar
  • 1 tsp dry Yeast
  • 2 cup refined Flour
  • 1 tsp + 1 tsp Olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp Salt

For Toppings -

  • 7 slices processed Cheese
  • 2 tbsp Pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup Mozzarella Cheese
  • 3 slice Tomato
  • few chopped Capsicum
  • few chopped Onion
  • 7 pieces Jalapeno
  • 10 pieces Olives
  • 1/4 tsp mixed herbs
  • 1/4 tsp Chilli flakes

Instructions :

  1. Place Pizza dough over Pizza plate or tray.
  2. Flatten the dough by stretching with both hands.
  3. Further, leaving a cm or more and create a dent.
  4. Then prick with centre of dough.
  5. Place the processed Cheese slices inside leaving the sides.
  6. Take the rolled dough and roast on hot tawa.
  7. Half cook both sides making sure not to cook completely.
  8. Place the half cooked rolled dough over Cheese placed Pizza base.
  9. Get the sides closer and seal tight.
  10. Furthermore, spread Pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly.
  11. Then spread 1/2 cup of grated Mozzarella cheese over the base.
  12. Top with tomato slices, chopped capsicum, onions, jalapeno and olives.
  13. Sprinkle 1/4 tsp Chilli flakes, 1/4 tsp mixed herbs.
  14. Further, bake in a preheated oven at 500 degrees fahrenheit or 200 to 250 degree celsius for about 15-20 minutes.
  15. Finally, slice the Cheese burst Pizza and top with Chilli flakes and Oregano.

Serve with your favorite dip!
